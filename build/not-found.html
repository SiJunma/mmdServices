Alana Eckert (Thomas)

Managing Partner

Alana joined MMD in 2012. Along with her charming personality, she brought innovation and progress into our group. It was clear right away that staffing was not a bus stop for Alana — it was her life from day one. She is known in the industry as a one stop shop for career support.

When clients hire her to staff a project or find a specialist for their team, she takes a holistic approach. She asks the right questions to gage how this prospective resource would impact her client’s career and looks for avenues to maximize that impact. Before even approaching our Recruiting Team, Alana would work with a hiring manager on an all encompassing candidate profile, including desired skills, industries, corporate culture mix, and work attitude. No detail is too small. She will work with our recruiters to carefully select a strong candidate, whose goals and ambitions would fall inline with her client’s needs. She will never compromise, because she wants you to win! Alana sees her clients as partners and this philosophy is the base of her continued success.

Internally, Alana is a part of our strategic decisions, she mentors newcomers, introduces the news tools and methodology to the team.

In the world of IT, she knows a million people, who actually genuinely like her! Alana smiles all the time, loves live music and appreciates a nice glass of whiskey! She is easy to talk to and hard to forget!
